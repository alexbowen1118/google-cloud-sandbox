WEDI Superintendents,

 

If you'll remember there were guest speakers at the Superintendent's Conference from the NC Green Travel Initiative.  Many of your parks should eligible for recognition from this program. 

 

Their website contains a lot of good information for suggestions and tips on sustainable practices for cleaning, energy reductions, water efficiency, landscaping  and even making your park festivals green.  It could be a valuable resource.

 

Please consider applying for recognition from the NC Green Travel Initiative.  The application process is simple and is available online.  Once recognized, your park will be eligible for an onsite consultation to help your parks cut energy use, reduce waste, conserve water and save money (which you can then use to spend in other line items of your park budget).  Also, your park will be advertised on the NC Green Travel website.  NERI recently completed the application process and was rated with the highest rating possible.

 

Here is the website:

 

http://portal.ncdenr.org/web/deao/ncgreentravel
