To help make your ordering a little easier and to cut down on the amount of requisitions via E-Procurement, you may now use your p-card with DOC.  However, their are some stipulations as follows:

  1. Prescription Safety Spectacles _can not be_ purchased or paid with
     p-card  (must complete purchase request for these no matter what
     the cost).
  2. Furniture of any kind what so ever _can not be_ purchased or paid
     with p-card ( must complete purchase request for these no matter
     what the cost).
  3. Filing, Storage, Gun or Any Type of Cabinets _can not be_
     purchased or paid with p-card (must complete purchase request for
     these type of items no matter what the cost).
  4. Business Cards _can not be_ purchased or paid with p-card (can
     only be ordered via the DPR District Offices ,see Find database
     #67 Ordering Business Cards for Park Field Staff).
  5. Printing of any kind _can not be_ purchased or paid with p-card
     (all printing must go through DPR PIO office).   6. Items that can't be shipped within 2 to 3 weeks _can not  be_
     purchased or paid with p-card (must complete purchase request for
     anything over this time limit).
  7. Purchases over the $2,500 dollar purchasing limit _can not be_
     purchased or paid with p-card (must complete a purchase request
     for anything over this amount).

DOC now has a shopping cart type ordering system and after you place an order; a representative will call you to finalize the order.  You can call DOC direct as well.
This new procedure should be very helpful to you especially when ordering motor oil, paint, janitorial supplies, etc. Please do not abuse this new privilege!!  Anyone who abuses this privilege or purchases/pays for anything covered in items 1 through 7 will have their p-card permanently revoked.
Thanks and Happy Ordering,
Tammy Dodd
DPR Budget Officer
